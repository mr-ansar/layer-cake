 Final result is that there are 2 services running - nut-server and nut-monitor.
 The first one interfaces with the UPS to acquire its current view of the world
 and the second one monitors the first. If it sees that power is about to come
 to an end (i.e. there is no mains power and the battery is about to fail) then
 it initiates a shutdown on the local host.
 
 The command history reflects multiple attempts to get the pair of services
 working properly. Some of the difficulties were definitely due to following
 an installation procedure that was obviously out-of-date. The architecture
 of "nuts" has clearly changed along the way.
